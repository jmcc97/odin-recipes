Spaghetti

Description

This classic recipe is simple, but never fails to hit the spot!

With spaghetti noodles, ground beef, onion, and tomatoes, you can indulge in this timeless dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups water
